Backlog Refinement

  • Prioritize the product backlog
  • Ensure backlog items are clear, well-defined, and estimated
  • Align backlog with business and customer needs
  • Collaborate with the team to clarify acceptance criteria

Sprint Planning

  • Forecast team velocity
  • Estimate task durations
  • Define and commit to Sprint Backlog
  • Set a clear Sprint Goal

Sprint Execution

  • Conduct Daily Stand-Ups (DSUs)
  • Collaborate to remove blockers and ensure progress
  • Maintain team cohesion and alignment toward sprint goals
  • Ensure visibility and communication within the team

Sprint Review

  • Demonstrate the product increment to stakeholders
  • Gather feedback for future improvements
  • Assess achievement of Sprint Goals and deliverables

Sprint Retrospective

  • Identify areas for team and process improvement
  • Discuss lessons learned from the sprint
  • Implement actionable items for continuous enhancement

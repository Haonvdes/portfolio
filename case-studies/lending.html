Customer Journey Map

Pain Point & Solution

Pain Point

• It takes around 3-5 hours for the 4-stage process.
• 8 signatures are required by customers..
• Consumer's information was not utilised.
• The rate of returning customers decreased.
• Cash must be borrowed at office.

Solution

• Reduce to 2 hours the workflow.
• Using e-signature via one time passcode.
• Optimize the application's client data..
• Promote advertising and promotions by applying.
• Cash paid straight to the account crediting online
